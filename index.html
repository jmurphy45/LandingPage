About Us

re at the Growler Memphis we celebrate craft beer. We have 24 beers on tap for you to choose from. Whether you want to stay for a pint or a flight or take a growler to go, we have what you need. You will love the rustic-sophisticated atmosphere. We are only a credit/debit establishment, we do not accept cash payment. Food will be coming soon. We hope to see you stay for a pint, leave with a growler. CHEERS!
